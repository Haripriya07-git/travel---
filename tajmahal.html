Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is one of the most iconic landmarks in India. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of love and architecture.

Popular Places to Visit Near Taj Mahal:

Agra Fort

A historical fort that served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors.

Mehtab Bagh

A beautiful garden offering a perfect view of the Taj Mahal.

Fatehpur Sikri

An ancient Mughal city with stunning architecture and history.
