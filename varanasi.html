Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi, is one of the oldest cities in the world and a significant religious hub. The ghats, temples, and vibrant cultural heritage make it a must-visit for history lovers and spiritual seekers.

Popular Places to Visit Near Varanasi:

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on the banks of the Ganges.

Dashashwamedh Ghat

One of the most iconic ghats of Varanasi, known for its evening Aarti ceremony.

Sarnath

A major Buddhist site where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon.
