Manali

Manali, a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh, is known for its stunning landscapes, adventure sports, and pleasant weather. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Popular Places to Visit Near Manali:

Solang Valley

Famous for adventure sports like skiing, paragliding, and zorbing.

Hadimba Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Hadimba, surrounded by cedar forests.

Rohtang Pass

A breathtaking high-altitude pass offering panoramic mountain views.
