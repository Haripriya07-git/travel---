Kerala - God's Own Country

Kerala, located in the southern part of India, is known for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. It is a top tourist destination offering a perfect mix of nature and tradition.

Popular Places to Visit in Kerala:

Alleppey Backwaters

Famous for its houseboats and scenic beauty, a must-visit for nature lovers.

Munnar

A hill station known for its tea plantations and breathtaking landscapes.

Kovalam Beach

A popular beach destination perfect for relaxation and water activities.
