Goa

Goa is a top beach destination known for its stunning coastline, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. Whether you want to relax on the beaches or enjoy thrilling water sports, Goa has it all.

Popular Places to Visit in Goa:

Baga Beach

One of the most famous beaches in Goa, known for its nightlife and water sports.

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea.

Dudhsagar Waterfalls

A breathtaking waterfall located on the Goa-Karnataka border.
