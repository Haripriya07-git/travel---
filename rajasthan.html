Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the Land of Kings, is known for its rich history, majestic palaces, and grand forts. It is one of India's most popular tourist destinations, offering a glimpse into royal heritage and vibrant culture.

Popular Places to Visit in Rajasthan:

City Palace, Jaipur

A magnificent palace that showcases the blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture.

Jaisalmer Fort

One of the largest forts in the world, known for its golden hue and intricate carvings.

Lake Palace, Udaipur

A stunning palace situated on Lake Pichola, offering a romantic and royal experience.
